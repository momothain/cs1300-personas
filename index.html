Personas: Self-Checkout UI

Study of the use and effectiveness of grocery store self checkout interfaces

Questions

  1. Why choose self checkout?
  2. Are you in a rush/trying to save time?
  3. Does this support that? Is there anything in the way
  4. Do you ever feel frustrated? Is there something you consistently hate about this or general self-checkout interfaces?
  5. Does it error? Do you know why?
  6. Do you ever purposefully want help from an attendant? Forced into it?
  7. Does it ecnourage you to do anything you wouldn't without prompt? Buy more things? Put them away? Get discounts? Enter your phone number/card? Get a card?
  8. Are you comfortable entering in an item number or item name? How would you search it up if you could however you wanted?
